ID: US-05<br>
Category: Customization / Character<br>
User Story:<br>
As a player, I want to customize my character, so that I can personalize their appearance.<br>
Acceptance Criteria:<br>
1)Player can change character outfit (uniform)<br>
2)Player can equip and remove glasses<br>
3)Player can equip and remove headwear<br>
4)Changes are visible immediately<br>
5)Changes are saved after exiting the game<br>
6)Player can preview customization before applying changes<br>
